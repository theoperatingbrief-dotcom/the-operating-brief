Kimi Antonelli won the Miami Grand Prix for the third consecutive race, edging Lando Norris to extend his Formula 1 championship lead. Norris finished P2 and called himself gutted, while Charles Leclerc received a 20-second post-race penalty after his own error, and Max Verstappen a 5-second penalty. In the Premier League, Manchester United beat Liverpool 3-2 with Michael Carrick in the dugout, Richarlison headed Tottenham to a 2-1 win at Aston Villa to lift Spurs out of the relegation zone, and Inter Milan were confirmed as Serie A champions after Marcus Thuram sparked a win over Parma.

Craig Bellamy's diagnosis with a neurodegenerative disorder dominated the NRL conversation, drawing support from across the game. In Round 9, the Sharks ran over Wests Tigers 52-10, the Knights beat the Rabbitohs 42-38, and the Roosters accounted for the Broncos 38-24. NSW won Women's State of Origin Game 1, with Jesse Southwell's field goal and a Jessica Sergis try sealing the result.

Brisbane Lions beat Essendon 143-79 in the biggest AFL margin of Round 8, Sydney beat Melbourne 131-114 with new recruit Rosas starring at the SCG, and Adelaide edged Port Adelaide 76-75 in the SA derby. Collingwood and Hawthorn finished 93-93. In the NBA playoffs, the Philadelphia 76ers completed a series comeback against the Boston Celtics, and Victor Wembanyama posted a double-double as the Spurs clinched their series.

Cameron Young won the Cadillac Championship at Doral wire-to-wire on the PGA Tour, while Nelly Korda claimed her third LPGA title of 2026 with a four-shot win in Mexico. In cricket, Australia's women lead the ICC Women's ODI rankings with the Women's T20 World Cup 2026 squad confirmed this weekend. Next weekend sees NRL Round 10, AFL Round 9, and LeBron James' Lakers open their playoff series against the Oklahoma City Thunder.
