Week Preview

Today's Briefing

The biggest story hanging over this week is one that's been building for years — Aston Villa are in a European final. After seeing off Nottingham Forest in the Europa League, with Buendía and McGinn doing the damage, Emery's side now have a continental showpiece to prepare for. The city of Birmingham hasn't felt anything like this in a long time, and the question coming into the week is how Villa manage the weight of that expectation alongside their remaining domestic commitments.

In the NRL, nobody is running hotter than the Dolphins right now. A 44-12 win over the Bulldogs — 32 points, clinical from start to finish — puts the competition on notice. This week's fixtures will tell us whether there's anyone capable of matching their tempo, or whether Wayne Bennett has built something that's starting to look genuinely ominous. Fremantle carry similar energy out of the AFL after their shellacking of Hawthorn in Perth, and the Hawks will need a serious response this week if they don't want to fall dangerously behind the pace in a crowded top eight.

Formula 1 doesn't leave Miami without the sport fundamentally changing the conversation. Kimi Antonelli's record-breaking win is the kind of debut-season story that resets expectations entirely, and Toto Wolff responding publicly to critics tells you exactly how confident the Silver Arrows camp is feeling right now. The next race will tell us whether that Miami performance was circuit-specific or whether Mercedes have genuinely found another gear.

The cricket subplot is quietly significant heading into the weekend. With Cummins, Hazlewood, and Head staying in the IPL for the playoffs, Australia's ODI squad against Pakistan is going to look very different from what anyone expected. That creates real opportunity — and real scrutiny — for whoever gets the call-up. And off the field, the NHL salary cap jumping eight and a half million dollars to $104 million is about to force some fascinating decisions from every front office in the league, with free agency just around the corner.

Watch this weekend for whether Villa can keep that European high going, and whether Antonelli's Formula 1 breakthrough was a flash or the start of something the title contenders genuinely have to worry about.
